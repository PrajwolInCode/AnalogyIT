What I can set up for you

A clean, supportable setup that’s easy for you and your staff to use.

  • Business email (Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace) + domain connection
  • User accounts & staff access (sign‑ins, permissions, shared resources)
  • Onboarding/offboarding checklist (joiners/leavers done cleanly)
  • Devices (PCs, laptops, phones) set up with a simple standard build
  • Printers & scanning working reliably for day‑to‑day operations
  • Wi‑Fi & network basics checked (speed, coverage, stability, tidy cabling)
  • Security basics (MFA, updates, backups, recovery options)
  • Handover notes so you’re not stuck later

Minimum downtime, more uptime

I aim for a setup that just works — with fewer interruptions and faster fixes when something goes wrong.

1. Quick check

2. Build & test

3. Handover

You get tidy notes + simple “how to” steps, so you’re not dependent on guesswork.

Good for: tradies, cafes, consultancies, real estate, small offices — and any team that needs reliable email + staff access.
